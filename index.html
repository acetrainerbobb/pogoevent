Introduction

Welcome to the Kanto Gym Leader Challenge! This event brings together trainers from around the world to battle the iconic Gym Leaders of the Kanto region. Test your skills, earn battle points, and claim your place at the top of the leaderboard!

The event will run for one month, with the start date announced soon. Get ready to challenge some of the most legendary leaders in the Pokémon world!

Event Details

Welcome to the one-month Pokémon GO Gym Leader Challenge! This event will run for one month only. The start date will be announced later, and the event will run until [End Date].

All trainers are welcome to participate! To take part, simply battle the Gym Leaders, earn battle points, and collect badges. The more battles you win, the more points you earn. Compete to climb the leaderboard and become the ultimate Kanto Gym Champion!
